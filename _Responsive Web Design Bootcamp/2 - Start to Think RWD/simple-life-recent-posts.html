Race & Secularism in America: Some Musings on Vincent J. Lloyd's Work

Vincent J. Lloyd's 2016 book, Race and Secularism in America, is a groundbreaking book which analyses one of the most important, yet hidden, forces working in our world today: secularism. Where previous works have examined the relation between religion and the state, or race and the state, Lloyd reveals how secularism shapes and manages both of these entities and the effects are as drastic as they are invisible.
